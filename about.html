Our Story

Founded in 1985 in the heart of Kyoto, Sakura Dining is a celebration of traditional Japanese flavors and craftsmanship. Our founder, Chef Kenji Yamamoto, envisioned a place where the essence of Japanese hospitality — known as omotenashi — meets the finest culinary traditions passed down through generations.

From delicate sushi to hearty bowls of ramen, every dish at Sakura Dining tells a story of heritage, precision, and passion. Over the decades, we have grown into a destination restaurant cherished by locals and travelers alike.

Why Dine With Us?

Authentic Cuisine

We use only fresh, seasonal ingredients sourced locally and from Japan to ensure an authentic taste.

Cultural Ambience

Experience the atmosphere of a traditional Japanese setting with modern comfort and elegance.

Excellent Service

Our friendly staff uphold the spirit of omotenashi—serving every guest with genuine care.
